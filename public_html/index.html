Overview

In music, chords can be split up into 4 basic categories - Major, Minor, Diminished, and Augmented. Although there are many other types in between, these 4 make up the most broad and basic chord types.

According to Landr.com, Chords are two or more harmonic notes played at the same time. Most basic chords are made up of 3 notes, and are built starting with their root note. The rest of the notes depend on the type of chord that is being played. These notes create a dramatic difference in how the chord sounds. Each type of chord has a distinct sound that becomes more and more apparent as one gains more knowledge about them.
